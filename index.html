Science for Sustainable Earth

Using geospatial technologies, environmental science, and field-based knowledge to understand real-world environmental challenges and translate data into stories people can relate to and act upon. By combining scientific insight with local knowledge and community participation, we work to strengthen water security, climate resilience, clean air, and environmentally responsible development through research, capacity building, and meaningful Information, Education, and Communication (IEC) that connects science with everyday life.

About Us

GeoClimate Foundation is a not-for-profit organization working at the intersection of environmental science, geospatial technology, and community development.
